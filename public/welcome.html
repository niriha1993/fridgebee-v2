Three ways to add groceries
Your fridge, your way.
📷
Scan
Snap a receipt, a grocery app screenshot, or your open fridge. AI reads every line item — name, quantity, unit, category, and the printed price. No estimating.
Works with: Whole Foods · Trader Joe's · Walmart · Kroger · Tesco · Sainsbury's · Ocado · Coles · Woolworths · NTUC FairPrice · Cold Storage · Instacart · DoorDash · Amazon Fresh — and any grocery receipt photo.
🎤
Voice
Just say what you bought. AI transcribes and parses — with phonetic recovery built for local dialects and accents, not just standard English.
Global English plus local dialects: Singlish · Hinglish · Spanish · "courgette" / "zucchini" · "aubergine" / "eggplant" · "capsicum" / "bell pepper" — handled.
✍️
Type
Type naturally — "spinach, 6 eggs, 500g chicken breast, parmesan" — and the AI parser splits, normalises, and logs everything in seconds.
Per-item shelf life set automatically: spinach 3d · cilantro 4d · avocado 5d · eggs 21d · cheese 21d · butter 30d — not a flat 7-day default for everything.
